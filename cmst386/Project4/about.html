About Us

Our Story

It all started out with a simple belief - not all transactions have to be monetary. As the founder of SkillSwap, I found myself constantly astounded by the sheer amount of unique talents and skills the people around me possessed - but they often weren't being utilized to their full potential. Those skills, whether they be painting a masterpiece, coding an app, or cooking a five-star meal, hold tremendous value. Yet, too often, my friends and peers find themselves stuck doing the things that weren't their passion because it's what pays the bills. More often, my friends and I would discuss needing a service, but being unable to pay for it.

In 2023, I set out to redefine the way that we view those talents and skills. I envision a back-to-basics bargaining world, where people are able to freely exchange their unique abilities, all while fostering a sense of community and shared learning. From this vision - SkillSwap was born - a platform designed to unlock the potential of the type of community that we can build. A platform where your value isn't defined by dollars and cents, but by shared experiences and growth.
