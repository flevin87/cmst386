Community Guidelines

Respectful Interactions

We expect all members of our platform to treat eachother with respect and kindness. Any discrimination, hateful speech, bullying, or harassment will not be tolerated and may result in account suspension or termination

Legitimate Skills and Services

All skills and services offered and requested by users must be legal and ethical. We reserve the right to remove any of these which we deem as inappropriate or harmful. Listings or requests that do not follow these guidelines may result in account suspension or termination based on severity.

Account Security

Members are responsible for maintaining the security of their personal information. This includes never sharing your password with anybody and logging out of your account when you're done using Skill Swap, especially on shared devices.

Privacy and Confidentiality

Respect and privacy of others. Do not share or request personal information unless it is necessary for the skill or service being exchanged. All personal information should be treated as confidential.

Resolving Disputes

In the event of a dispute between users, we encourage open and honest communication to resolve the issue. In the event that an agreement cannot be made, please contact us through the links on our website and we will try to assist you with the matter.

Clear and Honest Communication

Skill Swap is meant to be a place where the community can grow together with trust and reliability. Be clear about your skills and services that you are offering, and what you expect in return. Misleading others can result in disputes and loss of community trust.

Quality of Services

Always strive for excellence in the services you provide to others. High-quality work strengthens our community and can lead to more successful swaps in the future.

Reporting Misconduct

If you encounter any behavior that violates our community guidelines, please report it to us immediately. We are dedicated to providing a safe and respectul environment, and we rely on our community to help us achieve this.

Commercial Activity

Skill Swap is designed for personal, not commercial, exchanges. Offering services in exchange for money is not allowed.

No Discrimination

Skill Swap is an inclusive platform. We do not tolerate any form of discrimination based on age, race, gender, orientation, religion, nationality, disability, or any other identifying characteristics.

No Illegal Activities

Any illegal activities are strictly prohibited on our platform. This includes, but is not limited to, offering illegal services, fraud, or soliciting illegal activities.
