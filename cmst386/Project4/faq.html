FAQs

How Does Skill Swap Work?

Skill Swap is an online platform that allows users to trade their unique skill sets with other users' skills in order to complete services for one another. Skill Swap works intuitevly by allowing you to swipe on user profiles in a stack based upon your needs. When two users have swiped right on one another, a connection is made and the users are notified. From there, users are able to discuss their needs and make a deal.

What kind of skills can be traded on Skill Swap?

Any skill or service can be traded on Skill Swap as long as they are legal and follow our Community Guidelines.

Is Skill Swap free?

Skill Swap is completely free-to-use! However, we do have a paid for version of the application that unlock more features if you would like to help support the developers.

What features are available under the premium version of the application?

By paying for the application, you will have access to unlimited monthly connections, the ability to filter users based on your needs, and opportunities to put your profile ahead of others. You'll also be the first to access any new features brought to Skill Swap.

How do I start trading?

All you need to do is Download the Skill Swap app on the App Store or Google Play and register your account to start trading today!
