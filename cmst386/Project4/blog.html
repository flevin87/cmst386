The beginning

This idea came to me doing something very simple. I made an offer to a coworker to help design and build a website when I felt confident enough because he offered to trim my trees in my front yard. He offered this for free as he doesn't value money the same way that a lot of people tend to. This got me thinking about the potential of bringing back bartering in a way that was easy to access and simple to engage with. I discussed this idea with some friends and family, and eventually somebody sent me a TikTok which posed the question, "why doesn't an app like this exist yet", and I knew from there that developing this platform was something I wanted to do. I did not consider myself a professional coder, by any means, but I am fully committed to learning everything it takes to build this from the ground up.
