Responsive Design

Responsive design ensures that website layouts adjust in a dynamic way, responding to both screen size and orientation. This is crucial when developing content because users consume web content in significantly different ways, be it a smartphone, desktop, tablet, or laptop. By developing with responsive design in mind, the developer is able to significantly improve user experiences, increase engagement, and boost consumer retention and conversion.

Audience Analytics

Google Analytics provides valuable information about a target audience and how they interact with a developer's or business's content. Here are some key reports that one can get from Google:

Audience Overview

This is an overall snapshot of who is accessing your website. This is important to give you a sense of who your users are. In it are details regarding age, gender, interest, amongst other demographics. Additionally, you can monitor traffic performance in nearly realtime. Using this report can allow you to adapt quickly, so that you may adjust and tailor your content and marketing strategies to suit the users.

Acquisition Overview

This allows you to view what channels, or source, your target audience is coming from. By analyzing your best channels, you are able to adjust your focus and optimization on them to guarantee higher conversion rates and therefore a better ROI.

Behavior Flow

The behavior flow report helps you understand the actual paths that users take on your website from the time they enter until the time they leave. This allows you to see what content keeps your users engaged and where they usually go from there. Understanding the behavior flow report can allow you to understand content issues or where users drop off, so that you can then optimize these areas of your content.
